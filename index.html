Dress to Impress With a Tie

Wearing a tie is the best way to look sharp and taken seriously in any setting. Whether you're going on a job interview, wedding, or just trying to make a fashion statement; a tie will enhance your look and receive compliments from people about your appearence. But before you can make this a part of your wardrobe, I will teach you how to tie a tie first.

How to Tie a Tie

1. Start with the wide end of the tie on the right and the small end on the left draped around your neck with the collar flip up. Begin with the small end slightly above your belly-button. Only move the active wide end.

2. Wide end over the small end to the left.

3. Under the small end and the right.

4. Across the front and to the left.

5. Up into the neck loop from underneath.

6. Down through the loop you've just made in the front.

7. Thighten the knot by pulling down on the wide end. Slide the knot up and adjust.

Dress to Impress With a Bow Tie

Once you've master tying a tie and implemented into your style, you can take it to another level by wearing a bow tie. A bow tie is a perfect addition to wear at formal events, but if you want to stand out in the office amongst your co-workers, then a bow tie will do the job. Follow these steps below to tie a bow tie to look like Bill Nye the Science Guy.

How to Tie a Bow Tie

1. Start with one side alonger than the other draped around your neck with the collar flip up.

2. Cross both sides and tuck the longer end up and over to make a easy knot.

3. Fold the shorter end into the shape of a bow, pressing against your neck.

4. Pull the long side over the bow and then pull both sides of the bow together.

5. A hole will form in the bow near your neck, a curve shape behind the bow section, Fold the middle of the loose end of the bow tie and push through the curve shape.

6. Adjust the bow tie until it forms the shape you want.

Where to Buy

Now that you've learned how to tie a tie and bow tie, it's time to buy to start your collection. They both come in different colors, styles and fabrics. Instead of worrying about whats in style, choose something that makes you confortable and have fun with it. You can buy a tie or bow tie at any department store like Macy's, Nordstrom, or Men's Wearhouse.
